This is me, Biktimirkin Evgeniy Yurievich in 2015

Born in Kuibyshev, USSR on November 26, 1988. Not to say that I liked studying, however, I managed to find everywhere something interesting. In addition, now I am an engineer with a degree in "Management and informatics in technical systems". For a long time I worked as an electrical designer - figuring out how to make "living" out of non-living. And now I'm a software engineer, poking around in every language our department needs.

I started my creative path with the novel "Territory of Chaos". It was published in Samara 03.27.2011. Now the second version of this novel is ready.

In the same year, the novel "Introvert" was published by the "Samara Regional organization of young writers" in the fall. Thoughts in a novel are valuable, so I've been thinking for a long time about rewriting it someday. But since I've already finished the whole story, I'd rather concentrate on more pressing matters...

I also wrote the novel "Apostles" in 2012, many stories. A few times I was asked to write articles and texts - of course, I was glad to help. The story "Parity" was published in the magazine "Youth Wave" No. 17 dated 12.15.2016 in Samara.

I have not written for a long time, although sometimes I write down beautiful ideas.
